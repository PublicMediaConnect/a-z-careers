Career: Video Producer

A Video Producer is involved in all aspects of making a video, including: writing scripts, identifying locations for filming, setting a schedule, developing a budget and hiring the crew and actors. They must make sure that the creative and production processes run smoothly, on time and on budget This career requires a Bachelor’s Degree. To prepare for this career you might develop your presentation skills, study commercials to identify a producer’s style and the central message, or make you own video shorts using your own device and free video production software. Potential salary: $69,000/year.
