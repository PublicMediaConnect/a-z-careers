Career: AEROSPACE ENGINEER

Aerospace Engineers use their knowledge of math and science to design airplanes, spacecraft, satellites, and UAV’s. They also test and imagine designs that improve our exploration of air and space. This career requires at least a Bachelor’s Degree. To prepare for this career you might experiment with paper airplanes and model airplanes, learn computer programming basics or coding, and use math and problem-solving skills to identify and solve real-world problems. Potential salary: $103,000/year.
